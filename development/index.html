1. Same-Day Shipping

In certain cities there is an option of getting a FREE 1-day shipping, as well as the FREE same day shipping! These are the options that usually cost a lot! In order to qualify for those you need the following:

• Total value of your order needs to be at least $35.
• Only same-day eligible products can be delivered in this way. The same stands for the products delivered using the 1-day shipping.
• You need to finish the purchase (checkout) before the cut time, which is usually at noon.
• Your shipping address needs to be on the list of eligible destinations for these options. There are over 8000 cities in the continental part of the US that are eligible for these options.

2. Sharing Prime Membership

Sharing the benefits of your Amazon Prime membership with your family is actually a thing! You can do it with up to 4 people without any fear for your account security! Namely, you can share your shipping benefits with 4 people you choose without sharing your account details! In fact, sharing those would be a breach of Amazon Terms of Service!

There is a catch to it though – you can share the benefits only with the people who live at the same address as you. This way, Amazon makes sure that this is available only for the family members. The sharing process is as simple as it gets – all you need to do is to enter their name, birthday, and email address and send the invitation. In order to accept it, the household member needs to enter your birthday, and the process is finished!

3. Prime Photos

Regular Amazon customers have 5GB free data storage at their disposal. They can use this space for any data really. Pictures, videos, audio files, or any other data you wish to save can be stored there.

Amazon Prime members has an expanded version of this plan. Not only that you will get the aforementioned 5GB, but you will also get the Prime Photos – an option that allows you UNLIMITED photo storage! The only thing to keep in mind is that you need to upload photos that are smaller than 2GB, and that the format of the photo is supported.
This program is not meant for commercial use!

4. Prime Video

Amazon Prime also brings the Prime Video – service that is on par with the leaders in the entertainment industry! It competes with Hulu and Netflix and that says a lot about the quality of the shows provided there.

This service is expanding rapidly thanks to the Amazon Studios. This branch of the company is rapidly producing high quality content, and the level on which everything is produced is supreme. This production has even won 2 Academy Awards so far!
This service can even be extended with the other premium channels such as PBS KIDS, HBO, Anime Strike, Horror TV, and various other options.

6. Twitch Prime

Twitch is the best known platform for streaming video games. If you are a fan of these, you will enjoy this service to the full extent! It is very similar to YouTube, but with the difference that these are all live streamed games.

Twitch is free, but the Turbo version has some perks that are not available for free users. Twitch Prime is even better, as it allows all the features of the Twitch Turbo, but there are also monthly free downloads of commercial video games!

If you are a regular user of Twitch, upgrading to Twitch Prime will come with a free 30-days trial!

7. Prime Pantry

Prime Pantry is all about the kitchen! Here you can order groceries, kitchen accessories, and that includes some items that are not available anywhere else.

This service comes with 2 different shipping options: you can either pay $7.99 for each delivery, or you can make a monthly subscription that is $4.99 and gives you a free shipping for all orders over $40. Due to the sensitivity of the items included in this portfolio, the air shipping is not available, and the usual delivery time is 4 business days.

8. Lending Library

Lending Library allows people who own Kindle Fire tablets or Kindle e-readers to rent the books for FREE! There are no restrictions in terms of sharing or the time that you are allowed to keep the book, but you are allowed to borrow just 1 per calendar month. Still, it comes free of charge.

There is also the Prime Reading service that offers over 1000 books in this format. Finally, there is the Kindle First program that allows downloading a free book every month to all Prime users. It often includes titles that haven’t been published in hard copy yet!

9. Free Prime For Months

For any delivery that comes too late you are eligible for a full month of free Prime subscription. All you need to do is to contact their support and the problem will be solved and you will get compensated!

Note: If you received free shipping through Amazon Prime, you may be eligible for a free one-month extension when promised delivery that isn’t met. Prime Extensions (including Mom) are limited to one per free trial and 12 for an annual membership. Free Amazon Student Membership do not qualify for this extension.

On top of that, there is a Prime Student program that allows you getting free 6-months trial for Prime. It does apply only to students who have valid .edu email addresses. Once the trial is over, you can subscribe for the extension at just 50% of the price.

Note: This free trial does not include all of the features of the standard Amazon Prime subscription.

10. Prime Day and Lightning Deals

Lightning Deals is all about specific products being available at discounted prices in limited quantities and for a short period of time.

This program is not exclusive to Prime, but there are some perks to it. You will get a 30-minutes head start over all the other users for most such deals. You can visit this page to check what is in stock at any time. Finally, there is the Prime Day which happens once every year but brings thousands of products at discounted prices and in large quantities.
